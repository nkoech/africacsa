About this map

This interactive map was developed with the sole purpose of providing visualization of the total number of farming households in Africa by 2015. The data was derived from Afripopâ€™s 2015 raster layer by first subtracting urban population and then dividing the result (rural population) by the average household size in Africa which is approximately five people.
