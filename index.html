About the Application

This interactive map was developed with the purpose of providing visualization of the total number of farming households in Africa by 2015 and the related climate scenarios in different years. Population data was extracted from Afripopâ€™s 2015 raster layer while the RCP emission scenarios were derived from the Climate Wizard web mapping application. Mapbox Studio was used to style the map. Credits: Climate Change, Agriculture and Food Security (CCAFS)

How to use

Click on a country/region to view its predicted emission scenario in different years. Zoom in to view regions.
